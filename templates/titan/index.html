The Problem

The Opioid Project is utilizing data to help stem the opioid epidemic. The number of drug-involved overdose deaths has more than tripled since 1999 with the majority of deaths caused by opioids. This corresponds with the increase of opioid prescriptions in the late 1990’s and early 2000’s before it became evident that they could be highly addictive. In 2017, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services declared the Opioid Epidemic a public health emergency.
